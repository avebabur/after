DEAR READERS

Let me provide a brief overview of my book titled "After." The reason for this title is that four years ago, a wonderful person came into my life. Since this girl appeared, many things began to change. Therefore, the ideology of this book revolves around the situations that occurred after she entered my life. The idea to write this book came to me two years ago. At that time, I would jot down notes on my phone. After another two years, I have finally completed writing the book today.

TO SUMMARIZE

the book is about me and the girl I fell in love with. It briefly recounts what happened between us over a period of 2 years and 117 days. The book describes actions from 2021 to 2024. (You will be able to read the full story in the book for a complete understanding.)

PURPOSE FOR WRITING

My purpose for writing is that during the time we were talking, we had many plans together, one of which was to write this book. So, even though we don't talk now, I want to ensure that this plan is realized by writing and publishing it. Another reason is that her arrival in my life sparked my interest in writing, even though I wasn't initially interested in reading.

LOVE CHANGES A PERSON

During the three months I spent writing this book, I experienced various emotions. Sometimes, events struck me deeply, and at other times, I found myself reminiscing and feeling nostalgic. After all, we are human; we often miss and think about people from our past. Until now, I had never loved a girl like this. Perhaps because of that love, I began to change in ways I never expected. On one hand, this person has brought significant benefits to my life; she helped me discover my hidden talents and encouraged my growth in many areas. One of these talents is playing the piano. I fell so deeply in love with her that, knowing she liked boys who could play the piano, I started learning it myself.
